What I do

FAQ

Where do I start?

If you are reading this, we can say you already have. The next step is to get in touch. I'll contact you ASAP to guide you or answer further questions you may have.

What are the services you offer?

I offer Web Design and Development, Website Review and I serve as a public speaker

How often will I hear from you?

In the beginning, every two or three days during the first week or two. Then, at least once a week I'll have updates about the project.

How will I see the updates?

I use Trello for project management. You'll also have access to a private repository so you easily see how the project is developed on a daily basis

How will we communicate?

I prefer email or Skype. We can also have a phone call if the need arises

Do you do public speaking?

I love the opportunity to speak about things that exite me. I'm also remarkably competent being on the spot. Thanks to Toastmasters I'm able to connect with people even if they are not tech-savvy.

How will my project be managed?

Every project project have special needs but I tend to follow double diamond process. The phases it goes through are define discover, develop and deliver. Get in touch to see how your project fits into the process.
