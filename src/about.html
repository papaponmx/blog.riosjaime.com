About Me

I am a web developer who specialises in Web Design, Front End Development, Websites Optimization. I also enjoy enhancing the UX with menaingful animations.

I help online businesses, entrepreneurs, users and organisations reach their goals faster, by developing faster, useful and responsive websites.

About what I do

I see like this: Websites are solutions to people's problems. A brilliant solution to the wrong problem can be worse than no solution at all, therefore I find the right problem and then fulfill human needs.

I develop fast and interactive websites. I use the latest technologies on my workflow, so I can focus on what matters, like improving website performance, universal accessibility, best practices and web standards.

FAQ

What are the services you offer?

Right now I offer Web Design and Development, Website Review, this includes WordPress themes and Jekyll powered static sites.

Are you avaliable?

Please contact me to check my current availability, this can change daily and usually book a few weeks in advance. Please still contact me if your project requires urgent attention.

How often will I hear from you?

In the beginning, every two or three days during the first week or two. Then, at least once a week I'll have updates about the project.

How will I see the updates?

I use Trello for project management. You'll also have access to a private repository so you easily see how the project is developed on a daily basis

How will we communicate?

I prefer email or Skype. We can also have a phone call if the need arises

How will my project be managed?

Every project project have special needs but I tend to follow double diamond process. The phases it goes through are define, discover, develop and deliver. Get in touch to see how your project fits into the process.

Where do you work from?

I usually work from a laptop at home/office or next to the beach depending on the city I'm visiting. I'm might relocate or visit your facilities if the need arises.

Is there a legal contract to sign?

All clients are required to sign a Contract of Works, this outlines the agreed hours, budget or fixed cost of the project as well as the scope and time line. NDA agreeements carry a fixed cost on top of any agreed hours.

What is your rate?

All work is billed on an a day rate basis with consulting-only services at a fixed price. If your project requires a fixed price this is possible. My rates are non negotiable. My favorite introduction is "Hi James. We have a budget of X for X what could you do in that budget"

What is currency do you prefer?

I always bill in USD.

Do you do public speaking?

I love the opportunity to speak about things that exite me. I'm also remarkably competent being on the spot. Thanks to Toastmasters I'm able to connect with people even if they are not tech-savvy. Please contact me for more information.

Where do I start?

If you are reading this, we can say you already have. The next step is to contact me to discuss your project.

If you further questions or want to discuss an awesome project you can send me an email to hola@riosjaime.com.
